# Shading proposal indicators — PoliMi Città Studi

Equal implementation budget per solution: **EUR 200,000** · trees scenario: 166 semi-mature trees · sails scenario: 31 modules of 8x8 m

'In shade' = less than 4 h/day of direct June-August sun. Surface temperature is a clear-sky afternoon proxy: T = 26 degC + 4.3 x absorbed direct solar (kWh/m2/day), mixed over a ~10 m neighbourhood; ground cover from the DBT 2D survey. Costs are placeholder estimates.

The campus scenarios are *seeded*: 5 trees and 2 sails are forced within 30 m of each proposal point before the rest of the budget is optimized globally. Cost of that constraint vs the unconstrained optimum: 0.1% of total benefit for trees, 2.1% for sails.
